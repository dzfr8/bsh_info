Breed Overview

The British Shorthair is one of the oldest and most beloved domesticated cat breeds. Famous for their plush, plush fur and iconic round faces, these cats are known for their calm demeanor, intelligence, and affectionate–yet independent–nature. They're often described as teddy bears with whiskers!

  • Origin: United Kingdom
  • Coat: Dense, plush double-layered; soft to the touch
  • Colors: Most popular is “British Blue” (solid blue-gray), but comes in many others
  • Temperament: Calm, gentle, independent, affectionate but not clingy
  • Life Span: 12-20 years
  • Good for families: Yes! Very tolerant and safe with children

Fun Facts

  • The British Shorthair is the inspiration for John Tenniel’s famous illustration of the Cheshire Cat in "Alice in Wonderland".
  • They’re known to be quiet and not very vocal – perfect for peaceful households.
  • Their big, round copper or gold eyes and stocky build make them unmistakable and irresistibly cute!

Care Tips

  • Brush your British Shorthair weekly to maintain their thick coat.
  • Keep them at a healthy weight—they love lounging but benefit from interactive play!
  • Schedule regular vet checkups; this breed is generally very healthy.
